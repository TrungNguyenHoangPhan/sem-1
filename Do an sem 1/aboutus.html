ABOUT US

"We have a passion for design and
we will do everything possible to
help you find the perfect mirror"

After buying the house of our dreams and completing a top- to bottom renovation, we began to look for several mirrors to give our new home, the perfect finishing touch. After several evenings searching the internet it was clear that no mirror supplier offered beautifully designed mirrors at a sensible price, for the whole home. Therefore, we decided to take the matter into our own hands and that was the beginning of the Clear Reflections Shop.com

At The Online Mirror Shop.com we work directly with the designers and craftsmen, this allows us to only manufacture mirrors our customers want. We also miss out the middleman allowing us to offer a huge range of beautiful mirrors at an honest price, on top of that we have no expensive showrooms and instead focus our resources on our website and online team, ensuring you get a friendly and helpful service.
